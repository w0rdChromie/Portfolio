Urban City Champions (2021)

FIGHT!

Urban City Champions was an independent venture that I made with a couple of other students at the time. Made in Unity, using 2D graphics, this was our attempt at making a fighting game in a couple months. This game takes inspiration from games like Street Fighter III 3rd Strike, Dragon Ball Fighter Z, and Smash Brothers. Our collective decision was to design the game around a style over substance philosophy, and as such it does not put much emphasis on narrative, rather it focuses on its artistic vision and unique item-based gameplay. The game can be played here, and the Trello board can be accessed here.

    My contributions to this project were:
    • Prototyping builds in the Unity engine
    • Designing menu navigation
    • Creating art assets in Photoshop and similar applications
    • Playtester
    • Creating gameplay systems (moves, frame data, item system, health system, and clock system)
